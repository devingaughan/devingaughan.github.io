Hello, students, parents and friends!



I am a professional violin teacher and performer based in the Pasadena, California area.

Selecting the right teacher for your child is critically important because quality instruction gives students the opportunity to follow their love for music. As a result of my years of experience teaching violin, I have developed a truly unique teaching method unmatched by any other.

Every student has different abilities and needs. I tailor my lesson plans to each student’s interests and goals. To find out more about me and my class scheduling, you are welcome to browse the pages of my website. Offering a Variety of Classes
