Characters!

Devin Gaughan has been an illustrator and a writer since 5th grade.
His art style has been seen as very loose since he keeps the sketch lines in.
Devin is very skilled in many different genres such as steam punk and fantasy.
He has worked on a couple books under Odis Comics such as, TimeWorks,
My Complicated Life in Highschool, and a new book in the works called Voltina.

Isaiah Raya is a comic writer and illustrator since birth.
His First gig was Z comics with his friend, Owen Vandevelde.

Hes art style varies from robots to heroes fighting goblins in lands forgotten in time
